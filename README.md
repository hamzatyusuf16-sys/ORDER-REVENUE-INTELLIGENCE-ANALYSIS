# ORDER-REVENUE-INTELLIGENCE-ANALYSIS
This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze revenue performance, customer behavior, and pricing effectiveness.  The goal is to uncover key business insights that can support better decision-making in sales, marketing, and operations.
 OBJECTIVES
- Analyze revenue trends over time
- Identify high-performing regions and states
- Understand customer segments (age & gender)
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on sales
 TOOLS USED
- Tableau (Dashboard & Visualization)
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
 DASHBOARD FEATURES
- Revenue by Month (Trend Analysis)
- Revenue by State (Geographical Analysis)
- Revenue by Age Group (Customer Segmentation)
- Revenue by Category & Gender
- Revenue by Region
- Discount vs Quantity Correlation
KEY INSIGHTS
- Revenue shows strong seasonality, peaking in December (~$57.7M) while sales drop significantly around mid-year (September–October)
- Revenue is concentrated in a few high-performing states
- The South region contributes the highest share (~38%)
- Weak correlation between discount percentage and quantity sold
- Higher discounts do not consistently increase sales volume
- Customers aged 30–39 generate the highest revenue (~$44M)
- Younger age groups contribute significantly less
BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS
- Leverage Seasonality:
  Increase inventory and marketing efforts during peak periods (especially December)
- Target High-Value Customers:
  Focus marketing campaigns on the 30–39 age group
- Expand Market Reach:
  Invest in low-performing states and regions to improve coverage
- Optimize Pricing Strategy:
  Reduce excessive discounting and focus on value-driven pricing
